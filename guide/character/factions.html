Factions

As you adventure throughout the world of Cairn, you will discover that various groups of people have banded together to improve their chances of survival. These Factions are wary of outsiders and are not quick to trust. However, those that strive to help and who slay their enemies will find that these groups can become valuable allies in the battles ahead.

In Grim Dawn, Factions are groups of survivors with whom you can earn reputation. Reputation will grant you discounts at vendors, access to new quests and even special item rewards.

We are still actively expanding the Faction system, so stay tuned for more information on this exciting feature.
